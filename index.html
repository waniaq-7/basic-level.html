Benefits of Smart Working

How to do smart work?

Smart working is about more than just completing tasks. It is about choosing the best and most productive ways and technologies to achieve business goals.


ADVANTAGES OF SMART WORKING

  1. Higher employee retention
  2. Less stress, higher morale, and better employee wellbeing
  3. Manage their time autonomously

DISADVANTAGES OF SMART WORKING

  • Risk of Laziness
  • Infrastructure Costs
  • Reduced Visibility
